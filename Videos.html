The Celebration of Buwan ng Wika

The celebration of Buwan ng Wika, or National Language Month, is a vibrant and joyful occasion that honors the richness of Filipino languages and culture. Our section, Faith, takes part in various activities showcasing traditional Filipino attire, music, dances, and speeches that highlight the beauty of our heritage. We enjoy this month by immersing ourselves in cultural performances and artistic presentations that deepen our appreciation of the Filipino identity. Through engaging contests, like spoken poetry and storytelling, we celebrate the diversity of languages spoken across the Philippines. This month fosters unity and pride, reminding us of the importance of preserving our linguistic and cultural heritage.
